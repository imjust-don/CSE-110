Site Plan

My server is a powerful machine with a storage capacity of 10TB and 16GB of memory. It's equipped with an Intel i5 processor and a 1660 Super graphics card, making it capable of handling a wide range of tasks. I opted for a B450 motherboard for its reliable performance and affordability. To keep the system running cool and efficiently, I installed a custom water cooling loop. Additionally, I use Cloudflare to host my domain and DNS information, and Webmin as a management console with Cloudflared to create a network tunnel. Since I dont have access to my router settings.
