Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here for our website is to generate both general awareness and information about us and our company. In hopes that we might be able to generate more revenue We need this website because without it our ability to comunicate with potential customers is severly limited. This website would be a place for us to compile everthing a customer would need to know about us so that we can provide them the service.

Audience

This is for the people who might be interested in white water rafting but more specificly those who are interested in the services we provide as a rafting company And we will design our page to appeal to those people. We can do this by using fun fonts an colors water inspired color pallet and a straigh forward user interface. These people would be young thrill seakers looking for adventure. Financial status might be a little low and they want to go rafting. More than likely they'd be using smart phones and desktops to acces our site.
